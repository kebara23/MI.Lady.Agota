I document special gatherings—the energy on the dancefloor, the hush of a retreat, the spark of a celebration—so everyone who was there can feel it again, and everyone who wasn’t wishes they had been.

Services

Coverage and editing for organisers and hosts who throw unforgettable experiences—multi-day festivals, one-night parties, intimate retreats, community rituals and everything in between. Less posing, more what it actually felt like.

Photography

Dancefloors, ceremonies, fire circles and quiet golden-hour portraits—candid reportage with an editorial eye, ready to share with your guests and promote your next edition.

Videography

Highlights, aftermovies, vertical cuts and slow, human moments— edits with a cinematic pace so the atmosphere travels beyond the venue.

Tailored to you

I don’t sell one-size-fits-all bundles. Every festival, party and retreat has its own heartbeat—so we start as humans: you share what matters to you, your crowd and your vision, and we shape a single, clear offer around that conversation.

Whether you need stills, film, both, or something in between, we align on your priorities—timeline, energy on site, deliverables, how you want people to feel when they see the work—and I put that in writing before anything is confirmed. No rigid tiers: just an honest fit between what you need and what I can create for you.

What we’ll shape together

  • The story you want told (and what can stay private)
  • Schedule, locations and the flow of the day or weekend
  • Photo, video or both—and the formats that serve you best
  • How and when you’d like to receive the work
  • Any practical must-haves: access, safety, partners, languages

When it feels right, you’ll get a straightforward proposal you can say yes to—then we focus on being present for the real moments, not on fitting your gathering into a preset box.

    FAQ

    Answers that reduce friction before the form.

    How is the final price set?

    After we talk through your plans, I put together a custom quote based on duration, travel, what you need delivered, timelines and usage rights. Everything is confirmed in writing before we hold the date.

    How long does delivery take?

    Highlights typically 7–14 days depending on season; full galleries depend on volume. We align expectations in the proposal.

    How many revisions are included?

    We agree on rounds of feedback when we define your proposal—so it matches the size of the job, not a generic tier. Larger changes outside that scope can always be discussed separately.

    Do you work outside your city or country?

    Yes. Travel and accommodation are added when needed. Priority for multi-date blocks.
